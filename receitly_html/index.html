The Problem

Receipts are a nightmare for millions

From lost paper receipts to missed reimbursements, managing expenses shouldn't be this hard.

Clutter & Disorganization

Receipts scattered across emails, wallets, and drawers. Physical receipts fade, get lost, or become unreadable when you need them most.

Missed Reimbursements

Employees lose thousands annually by failing to claim business expenses. Misplaced receipts mean lost money and frustrated finance teams.

Financial Stress

Manual tracking makes budgeting impossible. Tax season becomes a scramble to find deductible expenses, leading to overpaid taxes.

Storage Issues

Physical receipts take up space and make it difficult to find purchases when you need them – for returns, warranties, or records.

Environmental Waste

Billions of paper receipts contribute to deforestation and waste. Most receipts contain harmful chemicals and can't be recycled.
