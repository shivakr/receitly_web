Terms and Conditions

Last Updated: 15 July 2025

Receitly is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app to upload, store, and manage receipts and financial documents.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Receitly, we may collect the following types of information to deliver and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and account details you provide during sign-up or profile updates.
  • Uploaded Content: Receipts, invoices, expense reports, and any files you upload to the platform for personal tracking and management.
  • Device & Usage Data: Information such as device type, operating system, app version, log files, IP address, and interactions with our app for troubleshooting and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected from you for the following purposes:

  • To provide core features such as receipt uploads, storage, and reporting tools.
  • To personalize and improve your user experience within the app.
  • To troubleshoot issues, enhance app performance, and ensure platform security.
  • To communicate with you about important updates, new features, and support-related matters.
  • To analyze aggregated usage trends to improve our services and offerings.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted cloud environments. We implement best-in-class security protocols to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel and trusted service providers who are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality.

4. Sharing of Information

Receitly does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers for cloud storage, analytics, and related operational services.
  • To comply with legal obligations, law enforcement requests, or court orders where applicable.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Receitly, our users, or others.

5. Your Rights

You have control over your data. At any time, you may:

  • Access and review the information you’ve shared with us.
  • Update or correct your personal details within your account settings.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to any legal obligations we must fulfill.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@receitly.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or to improve clarity. Any significant changes will be communicated via the app or by email.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your data, please get in touch:

Email: support@receitly.com
Website: www.receitly.com
